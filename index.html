What is Star Wars Legion?

Imagine you could fight alongside your favorite Star Wars character in a legendary battle: Han Solo, Darth Vader, Leia Organa, Padme Amidala, or even the notorious bounty hunter, Boba Fette. Well, here is your chance.

Star Wars Legion is a tabletop miniatures wargame by Atomic Mass Games that allows players to recreate battles from the Star Wars universe using small, highly detailed figurines. It's similar to games like Warhammer 40,000 or Age of Sigmar. In the game, you build and paint your armies, which can include iconic Star Wars characters, troopers, and vehicles. Each player takes turns moving their units and engaging in tactical battles on a game board. The objective is to complete mission objectives, defeat your opponent's forces, or achieve specific goals depending on the scenario you're playing. It's a fun way to immerse yourself in the Star Wars galaxy and engage in strategic warfare on your tabletop.


How to Find a Community?

Social media platforms like Facebook and Discord can be used to discover and connect with tabletop gaming communities dedicated to Star Wars Legion. On Facebook, search for relevant groups or pages related to the game, and you'll likely find discussions, event announcements, and fellow players. Discord, with its chat and voice channels, offers real-time communication with enthusiasts, making it easy to coordinate games and share strategies. Additionally, don't forget to check with your local game stores, as they often host tabletop gaming events and can connect you with like-minded players in your area. These combined efforts can help you dive into the exciting world of Star Wars Legion and expand your gaming network.

Alternatives to In-Person Gaming

If you're looking for alternative ways to enjoy Star Wars Legion, you can explore options like Tabletop Simulator, which allows you to play the game virtually with friends online, offering a digital tabletop experience. Another exciting option is creating role-playing scenarios within the Star Wars universe for fun storytelling adventures, where you can immerse yourself in the rich lore and create your own unique narratives. Finally, for those who enjoy the hobby aspect, collecting and painting the intricately detailed miniatures can be a satisfying and creative pursuit, even if you don't engage in gameplay. These diverse approaches cater to different interests and add depth to your Star Wars Legion experience.
